Research Yang Group

Materials and Photonics Lab

The Materials and Photonics Lab led by Dr. Ankun Yang seeks to develop novel materials and designs for use in next-generation energy efficient devcies.

The lab focuses on nanoscale fundamentals and scalable applications.
