Biography

Dr. Ankun Yang received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Materials Science and Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing China, in 2008 and 2011, and Ph.D. degree in Materials Science and Engineering from Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, USA, in 2016. He did postdoctoral training at Stanford University from 2016 to 2019. Dr. Yang joined Oakland University in 2020, where he is currently an assistant professor at the Mechanical Engineering department.

His research interests include:

  • Energy-efficient materials and devices: photonics, 2D materials, thermal management

  • High energy-density battery: Li-S battery

  • In-situ study of energy-related materials: optical microscopy and spectroscopy
