Ph.D. Students

Our research is interdisciplinary. Graduate students with background in materials science, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering or related fields are welcome to contact Prof. Yang. Please send a CV to ankunyang@oakland.edu with a brief statement indicating your research interests and related skills. Individuals with some of the following experiences are encouraged to apply:

  • Knowledge in electrochemistry and batteries

  • Knowledge in photonics, optical microscopy and spectroscopy

  • Experiences with FDTD simulations, COMSOL, Matlab, Labview

  • Hands-on experiences with lithography, cleanroom micro-/nano- device fabrication


Undergraduate Students

Highly motivated undergraduate students are welcome to participate in our research and publish their research results. Please contact Prof. Yang with a brief statement about your research interests and related skills.


Visiting scholars

Visiting scholars are welcome to contact Prof. Yang to discuss potential collaborations.
